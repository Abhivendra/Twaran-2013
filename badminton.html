• Badminton

    Introduction

    Loved by all irrespective of their age and origin, the love for this sport is the perfect demonstration of the phrase ‘Unity in Diversity’. TWARAN provides the perfect opportunity for all Badminton enthusiasts to prove their mettle in a breathtaking display of skill and agility as they battle it out in the state of the art indoor courts. It one of the all time favorite games for both boys and girls participating in TWARAN.

    Rules

    • Team events to be conducted.
    • At least 3 players required and maximum 5.
    • Teams having less than 3 players will be disqualified.
    • The sequence of a team game is as follows:
    • * 1st match: 1st singles
    • * 2nd match: 1st doubles
    • * 3rd match: 2nd singles (if required)
    • Each match is best of three games, 21 points each.
    • Against a team, only one player can play 2 matches.
    • In case of any controversies, the decision of the referees shall be considered as final.
