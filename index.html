    About Twaran 2013

    Sports are human life in microcosm, said Howard Cosell. Indeed sports are an inevitable part of a person's life. It brings in them a spirit to win, fervor to excel in what they do, instills a passion to fight and most importantly makes their mind sound.
    ABV-IIITM presents an opportunity to define your substance—Twaran 2013, the national level sports meet.

    Sportsmanship has always been a symbol of friendship and unity, thus the main motive of organizing such an event on a large scale is to increase the mutual understanding and to foster the co-operation level among various institutes. Sports and academics are not isolated from each other. The great energy level makes this event a lot more exciting not only to the players but also to the audience who witness their endeavor.

    Get your nerves right to witness the momensum Twaran is.
