    Introduction

    A new game called Mintonette evolved as a pastime to be played preferably indoors and by any number of players. The game took some of its characteristics from tennis and handball. Hand pumping lobs and elegant smashes make this game a jubilant watch and equally entertaining for the cheering viewers. The hard core muddy courts of TWARAN inculcate the pulse accelerating thrill and excitement.

    Rules

    • The format of the tournament is tournament is league cum knockout.
    • All league matches will be best out of three sets. Each set will be of 25 points each.
    • >All standard FIVB rules apply for both girls and boys.
    • Decision of organizers will be considered final and binding.
    • The schedule of matches will be displayed in time and is subject to change at any point of time.
    • If a team arrives late by more than 15 minutes, it will be awarded a 2-0(25-0, 25-0) loss.
    • The tie-breaking procedure for the Qualifying Tournament shall be the official USAV "Method One", as found in the 2008-2009 "Official USA Volleyball Domestic Competition Regulations".
    • In case of any controversies, the decision of the referees shall be considered as final.
